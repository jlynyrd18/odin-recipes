Katsu Pork

Description

A pork chop breaded and fried served with rice

Ingredients

  • Pork Chop
  • One egg
  • 1/8c of flour
  • Japanese Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Lay pork chops on flat surface with plastic wrap over
  2. Use tenderizing hammer to flatten pork chops
  3. Flip and flatten the other side of the pork chop
  4. Put flour into bowl, egg in another bowl whisked, and bread crumbs in a different bowl
  5. Put pork chop into flower coating it
  6. Put into bowl with egg and coat with egg on both sides, flip if needed
  7. Put into bowl of bread crumbs coating as much as possible, use hands to press crumbs onto pork chop
  8. Put into air frier for 20 minutes, flip after 10 minutes
  9. Rinse rice in a seperate bowl or pot that is going to cook in it two to three times, or until water is not murky
  10. Add water to bowl of rice cooker equal to amount needed listed on bag
  11. Set rice cooker to cook
