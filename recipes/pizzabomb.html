Pizza Bombs

Description

A bisciut rolled into a ball with marinara, cheese, and if wanted a topping.

Ingredients

  • Bisciuts
  • Marinara
  • Mozzerella Shredded Cheese
  • Topping like Pepperoni or hamburger

Steps

  1. Flatten bisciuts
  2. Add a 2 Tbsn of marinara
  3. Add a handful of cheese on top
  4. Add topping of choice
  5. Wrap the biscuit around the ingredients
  6. Put in air fryer upside down for 10 min at 350 degrees
